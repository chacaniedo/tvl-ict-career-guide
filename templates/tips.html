💡 Study & Learning Tips

1️⃣ Practice Every Day — Spend at least 30 minutes daily on small coding or tech activities.

2️⃣ Explore Free Online Courses — Visit sites like W3Schools, FreeCodeCamp, Coursera, or Khan Academy.

3️⃣ Use YouTube Wisely — Watch tutorials from creators who teach step-by-step.

4️⃣ Take Notes — Write what you learn in a notebook or digital note app to remember better.

5️⃣ Ask for Help — Teachers, classmates, and online communities can help when you're stuck.

6️⃣ Learn by Doing — Build small projects like websites, calculators, or digital posters to apply your knowledge.

7️⃣ Stay Organized — Manage your files, projects, and schoolwork using folders and naming conventions.
